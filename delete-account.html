Delete Your Account — Green Fingers

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open the Green Fingers app
  2. Go to the Settings tab (bottom right)
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted

Account deletion is immediate and permanent. You do not need to contact us.

What data is deleted

DataAction
Account (email, authentication)Permanently deleted
Plant collection (names, species, notes)Permanently deleted
Plant photosPermanently deleted from cloud storage
Care history (watering, fertilizing, etc.)Permanently deleted
Diagnosis historyPermanently deleted
Local data on your deviceCleared immediately

No data is retained after account deletion.

Can't access the app?

If you are unable to delete your account through the app, contact us at eyvindjorg@gmail.com and we will delete your account within 30 days.
